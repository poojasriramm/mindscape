Why do we need to pay attention to mental health?

Put a finger down if you've ever felt the low energy of your friend when they respond to your messages. Trust us; you're not alone.This pandemic outbreak was an enormous shock to everyone. A year has passed, but people are still adjusting to this setup. The pandemic forced us to keep our distance from our loved ones physically, and the “distancing” accidentally happened emotionally and mentally. Aside from taking care of our bodies, keeping our mental health in check is also essential. After all, our brain is the cheerleader that can encourage us to keep on carrying on.
